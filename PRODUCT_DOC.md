## 1. Company Overview

**Name:** Spazeo (pronounced spa-ZAY-oh)
**Domain:** spazeo.io
**Tagline:** "Step Inside Any Space"
**Category:** PropTech / Spatial Technology / Virtual Tour Platform

Spazeo is an AI-powered 360-degree virtual tour platform designed for real estate professionals and property managers. It transforms ordinary panorama photos into immersive, walkable 3D experiences -- no special hardware, no technical expertise required.

## 4. Product Overview

### What Spazeo Does
Spazeo takes 360-degree panorama photos (or even standard photos) and turns them into interactive virtual tours that anyone can walk through from their browser -- on desktop, tablet, or mobile.

### The Three-Step Promise
1. **Upload** your panorama photos
2. **Enhance** with AI (auto-analysis, virtual staging, descriptions)
3. **Share** with a single link -- embed anywhere, capture leads automatically

### What Makes Spazeo Different
- **AI-native from day one** -- Not an afterthought. AI staging, scene analysis, and description generation are core to the product.
- **No hardware lock-in** -- Works with any 360-degree camera or even smartphone panoramas. No $400+ proprietary camera required.
- **Gaussian Splatting** -- Advanced 3D reconstruction that goes beyond flat panoramas into true volumetric 3D.
- **Free tier** -- Get started with zero cost, zero credit card.
- **Built for real estate** -- Lead capture, analytics, MLS integration, and marketing tools purpose-built for property professionals.

## 6. AI Capabilities

Spazeo's AI features are what set it apart from every other virtual tour platform. All AI is built in and available without any additional tools or plugins.

### 6.1 AI Virtual Staging
Transform empty rooms into beautifully furnished spaces in under 30 seconds.

**How it works:**
1. Upload a panorama of an empty room
2. AI detects room type, dimensions, and lighting
3. Choose from 15+ interior design styles
4. Receive a photorealistic staged version

**Supported Room Types:** Living room, bedroom, kitchen, bathroom, office, commercial spaces

**Design Styles Include:**
- Modern Minimalist
- Scandinavian
- Industrial
- Mid-Century Modern
- Traditional
- Coastal
- Bohemian
- Luxury
- And more

**Why it matters:** Professional staging typically costs $2,000-5,000 per property and takes days. AI staging costs pennies and takes seconds.

### 6.2 AI Scene Analysis
Automatic quality scoring and improvement suggestions for every panorama.

**Quality Scoring (0-100):**
- Image Clarity -- sharpness, resolution, blur detection
- Lighting Quality -- exposure balance, dynamic range, over/under-exposed areas
- Composition -- framing, vertical alignment, camera placement
- Overall weighted composite score

**Auto-Detection:**
- Room type classification (living room, bedroom, kitchen, etc.)
- Object recognition (furniture, fixtures, appliances, architectural features)
- Material identification (flooring types, wall finishes, countertops)
- Spatial estimation (approximate room dimensions from the panoramic image)

**Actionable Feedback:**
- Flags issues: overexposed windows, blurry areas, poor framing, visible tripod, cluttered spaces
- Provides specific suggestions for improvement
- Track quality improvement over time with re-uploads

### 6.3 AI-Generated Descriptions
Compelling, accurate property descriptions written by AI from your panoramic photos.

**Capabilities:**
- Identifies room type, key features, and architectural style from the image
- Generates selling-focused copy that highlights the right features
- Fully customizable output

**Customization Options:**
- **Tone:** Professional, casual, luxury, conversational
- **Length:** Short taglines, medium listing descriptions, detailed long-form
- **Emphasis:** Natural lighting, spaciousness, architectural details, renovations
- **SEO:** Automatically includes relevant keywords for search ranking

**Example Output:**
> "Step into this sun-drenched open-plan living space, where floor-to-ceiling windows frame panoramic city views. The chef's kitchen features quartz countertops and premium stainless-steel appliances, flowing seamlessly into a generous entertaining area."

**Multi-Language Support:**
- Available now: English
- Coming soon: Spanish, French, German, Portuguese
- Planned: Mandarin, Japanese, Arabic

### 6.4 AI Floor Plan Generation
Convert panoramic views into structured floor plans.
- Automatic room detection from panoramas
- AI-generated layout with dimensions
- Interactive editor for refinements
- Links rooms to tour scenes for navigation

## 7. How It Works

### For Tour Creators (Agents, Managers, Photographers)

```
Step 1: Upload
   Drop your 360-degree panoramas or standard photos into Spazeo.
   Supports equirectangular, cubemap, and standard image formats.
   Bulk upload available for multiple scenes.

Step 2: Enhance
   AI automatically kicks in:
   - Analyzes each scene for quality and content
   - Classifies rooms and detects objects
   - Generates descriptions and tags
   - Suggests improvements
   You can then:
   - Add interactive hotspots to connect scenes
   - Apply virtual staging to empty rooms
   - Customize branding (logo, colors)
   - Set up lead capture forms
   - Arrange scene order

Step 3: Publish & Share
   One click to publish. You get:
   - A unique shareable link (spazeo.io/tour/your-tour)
   - Embed code for your website
   - QR code for print materials
   - Social media optimized preview
   Analytics start tracking from the first visit.
   Leads are captured and sent to you instantly.
```

### For Tour Viewers (Buyers, Renters, Guests)

```
1. Click the tour link (works in any browser, no app needed)
2. Navigate by dragging to look around in 360 degrees
3. Click hotspots to move between rooms
4. Use the mini-map to jump to any area
5. View on any device -- desktop, tablet, or phone
6. Optionally fill in a contact form to connect with the agent
```
